Matt Orehek is a self-taught freelance photographer from Seattle, Washington.

Born and raised in Seattle, he has spent years exploring the magnificent natural features that surround the city. He attended Claremont McKenna College in Southern California, graduating in May 2017 with dual degrees in Economics and Government. After visiting some of the most beautiful locations on the west coast, he turned to photography as a way to document his travels. First picking up a DSLR camera in January of 2017, his work focuses on human interaction with the living world. He has worked with both outdoor and active lifestyle brands.
